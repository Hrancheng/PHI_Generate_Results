TECHNIQUE: Standard 12-lead EKG was performed at a paper speed of 25 mm/sec and a gain of 10 mm/mV, using the Greenwich Cardiology Associates protocol.
FINDINGS:
- Rate: Sinus rhythm at 75 beats per minute
- Axis: Normal cardiac axis
- Intervals: PR interval is 160 ms, QRS duration is 90 ms, QT/QTc interval is 400/430 ms
- Morphology: No significant ST-segment or T-wave abnormalities are noted. Normal R wave progression.
- Conclusion: No acute ischemic changes.
IMPRESSION:
1. Normal sinus rhythm without evidence of acute ischemic changes.
2. Compared with previous EKG from 03/22/2021, no significant changes noted.
RECOMMENDATION:
Continue monitoring. Follow-up with cardiology recommended if symptoms persist or worsen.
