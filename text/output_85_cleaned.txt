PROSTATE ULTRASOUND dated 10/15/2021. INDICATION: Elevated PSA levels. 64-year-old male with a history of benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH). COMPARISON: Prostate ultrasound 10/12/2019. TECHNIQUE: Transrectal ultrasound was performed using a 7.5 MHz probe. FINDINGS: The prostate gland measures 4.2 cm x 3.9 cm x 4.7 cm and weighs approximately 45 grams. The central zone is homogeneously enlarged. There are multiple small calcifications throughout the gland. No focal lesions or suspicious areas of hypervascularity are noted. The seminal vesicles appear normal. Impression: IMPRESSION: 1. Benign prostatic hyperplasia without evidence of malignancy. 2. Stable size when compared to 10/12/2019.
