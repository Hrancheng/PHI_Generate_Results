Example 21:
CHEST CT WITH CONTRAST dated 09/27/2021. CLINICAL INDICATION: Evaluate for pulmonary embolism. TECHNIQUE: Helical CT of the chest was performed using 70 mL of Isovue 300 intravenous contrast without complication. Images were reconstructed at 1 mm slice thickness. FINDINGS: No evidence of pulmonary embolism. There is no pleural effusion or consolidation. The lungs are clear bilaterally without masses or nodules. The mediastinum is unremarkable. Mild cardiomegaly noted. The visualized upper abdomen is normal. IMPRESSION: 1. No evidence of pulmonary embolism. 2. Mild cardiomegaly without acute cardiopulmonary disease.
