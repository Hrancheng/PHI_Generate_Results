-The CT scan reveals dilated loops of small bowel consistent with partial small bowel obstruction.

-The liver, spleen, pancreas, and adrenal glands appear unremarkable.

IMPRESSION:  
1. Partial small bowel obstruction at the level of the ileocecal valve with associated moderate ascites. Further evaluation with follow-up imaging and clinical correlation recommended.  
2. No signs of bowel perforation or free intra-abdominal air.  
3. Unremarkable findings in liver, spleen, pancreas, and adrenal glands.
