PELVIC ULTRASOUND 3/21/2022 HISTORY: Chronic pelvic pain and suspicion of endometriosis. COMPARISON: Prior ultrasound 1/15/2020. FINDINGS: The uterus appears anteverted and is normal in size, measuring 8 x 4 x 5 cm. The endometrial stripe is homogeneous and measures 1.1 cm in thickness. The right ovary measures 3.6 x 2.5 x 3.2 cm and contains several small follicles. The left ovary measures 3.4 x 2.4 x 3.1 cm and also shows multiple small follicles. No adnexal masses or significant free fluid in the pelvis. No evidence of deep infiltrating endometriosis on ultrasound. IMPRESSION: 1. No evidence of adnexal masses or significant pelvic pathology. 2. Normal appearance of uterus and ovaries with multiple follicles. Recommendations for follow-up should be based on clinical symptoms.
