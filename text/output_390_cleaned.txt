ULTRASOUND OF THE ABDOMEN 03/15/2022 - CLINICAL INDICATION: Evaluation of abdominal pain and suspected cholelithiasis. COMPARISON: None. FINDINGS: The liver is normal in size and echo texture without evidence of focal hepatic lesions. Intrahepatic and extrahepatic bile ducts are not dilated. The gallbladder is visualized with multiple echogenic foci without shadowing, suggestive of gallbladder polyps. No evidence of cholelithiasis. The common bile duct measures 3 mm. The pancreas is homogeneous without evidence of masses. The spleen is normal in size. No free fluid is noted in the abdomen. The right kidney measures 11 cm, the left kidney measures 10.5 cm. Both kidneys have normal cortical thickness and echogenicity without evidence of hydronephrosis or stones. IMPRESSION: 1. Multiple gallbladder polyps without evidence of stones. 2. Normal examination of liver, pancreas, spleen, and kidneys.
