Sealed perforation suggested but no free air visible. Adnexa: Normal ovaries. No ovarian masses. Uterus: Normal size and contour with a small intramural fibroid. No free fluid in pelvis. Bowel: Otherwise unremarkable without evidence of obstruction or inflammation. IMPRESSION: IMPRESSION: 1. Mild ileitis likely reactive. 2. No evidence of acute appendicitis. 3. Small uterine fibroid, clinically insignificant.
