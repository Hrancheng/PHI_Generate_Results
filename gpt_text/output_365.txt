No free fluid or free air is present in the abdomen. Mild diverticulosis is noted in the sigmoid colon without signs of acute diverticulitis. 

IMPRESSION: 
1. No evidence of appendicitis.
2. Unremarkable examination of the liver, pancreas, spleen, and kidneys.
3. Mild diverticulosis of the sigmoid colon without acute diverticulitis.
4. Follow-up as clinically indicated for persistent symptoms.
