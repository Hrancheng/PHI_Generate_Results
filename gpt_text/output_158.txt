ABDOMINAL X-RAY [B-DATE]2/27/2023[O] CLINICAL INDICATION: Suspected bowel obstruction. HISTORY: Recent abdominal surgery and progressive abdominal distention. COMPARISON: Abdominal radiograph [B-DATE]2/24/2023[O]. FINDINGS: The abdominal radiograph reveals multiple dilated loops of small bowel with air-fluid levels suggestive of small bowel obstruction. No free air is visualized under the diaphragm. The colon appears decompressed. There are no calcifications or abnormal soft tissue masses noted. The osseous structures are intact. IMPRESSION: 1. Features are consistent with small bowel obstruction. 2. Recommendation for clinical correlation and potential surgical consultation if symptoms persist or worsen.
